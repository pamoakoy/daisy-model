\subsection{Peer reviewed articles}

Here we list peer reviewed articles where Daisy has been used as part
of the work presented.  Articles that merely refer to Daisy for
comparison or background are not listed.
\begin{enumerate}
\item \cite{ssoc}: Modelling of root {ABA} synthesis, stomatal
  conductance, transpiration and potato production under water saving
  irrigation regimes.
\item \cite{Boegh2009300}: Remote sensing based evapotranspiration
  and runoff modeling of agricultural, forest and urban flux sites in
  Denmark: From field to macro-scale.
\item \cite{eupotato}: Calibration procedure for a potato crop growth
  model using information from across Europe.
\item \cite{bgj-dom}: Challenges in modelling dissolved organic
  matter dynamics in agricultural soil using {DAISY}.
\item \cite{frantisek}: Dual permeability soil water dynamics and
  water uptake by roots in irrigated potato fields.
\item \cite{daisygis}: The Power of Models in Planning: The Case of
  {DaisyGIS} and Nitrate Leaching.
\item \cite{boegh04a}: Incorporating remote sensing data in
  physically based distributed agro-hydrological modelling.
\item \cite{daisy-somnew}: Calibration and Validation of the Soil
  Organic Matter Dynamics of the Daisy Model with Data from the
  {A}skov long-term experiments
\item \cite{daisy-init}: Initialisation of the soil organic matter
  pools of the Daisy model.
\item \cite{daisy-ems}: Daisy: An Open Soil-Crop-Atmosphere Model.
\item \cite{orgReviw}: A review of carbon and nitrogen processes in four soil nitrogen dynamics models.
\item \cite{mueller-aom}: Turnover of carbon and nitrogen in a sandy
  loam soil following incorporation of chopped maize plants, barley
  straw and blue grass in field
\item \cite{mueller-smb}: Temporal variation of {C} and {N} turnover
  in soil after oilseed rape incorporation in the field: Simulation
  with the soil-plant-atmosphere model Daisy.
\item \cite{smith97-compar}: A comparison of the performance of nine soil organic matter models using datasets from seven long-term experiments.
\item \cite{diekkruger95-compar}: Validity of agroecosystem models.  A comparison of results of different models applied to the same data set.
\item \cite{willigen91-compar}: Nitrogen turnover in the soil--crop system; comparison of fourteen simulation models.
\item \cite{daisy-fertilizer}: Simulation of Nitrogen Dynamics and
  Biomass Production in Winter Wheat using the {D}anish Simulation
  model Daisy.
\end{enumerate}
